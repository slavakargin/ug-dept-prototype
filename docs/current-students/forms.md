# Forms and petitions

This page collects common forms/petitions for current students. Procedures may change, so if you are unsure, contact advising first: (add contact).

## Common requests
### Prerequisite exception
Use when you are missing a prerequisite (or have equivalent preparation) and want permission to enroll.

- Form/location: (add link)
- What to include: transcript info, relevant grades, and a short explanation of preparation.

### Course substitution / requirement exception
Use when you want a course to satisfy a requirement in a way not automatically recognized.

- Form/location: (add link)
- What to include: course syllabus (or official description), and which requirement you want it to satisfy.

### Transfer credit / course equivalency review
Use when you took a course elsewhere and want it evaluated for BU equivalency.

- Form/location: (add link)
- What to include: syllabus, textbook/coverage, credit hours, and where/when taken.

### Track change
Use when you want to switch between tracks within Mathematical Sciences (e.g., Mathematics ↔ Actuarial ↔ Data Science & Statistics).

- Instructions: (add link)
- Advising strongly recommended before switching late in the program.

## Practical notes
- Many petitions require **supporting documentation** (syllabus, transcript, DegreeWorks screenshot).
- Allow time for review—do not wait until the add/drop deadline if possible.
